content: Frankfurt-listed shares of "Magnificent 7" stocks Apple and Amazon were broadly steady on Friday, in light volumes after both companies reported quarterly earnings after-market on Thursday.
Apple's Frankfurt listing was down just 0.1% after the company reported offered a <a href="/technology/apple-beats-wall-street-estimates-supported-by-iphone-sales-2024-10-31/">modest growth outlook</a>, even as early iPhone 16 sales grew faster than iPhone 15 sales. Apple shares were mostly in after-hours trading on Thursday.
Shares in Amazon listed in Frankfurt were up 1.3% after posting third-quarter <a href="/technology/amazoncom-beats-estimates-quarterly-revenue-2024-10-31/">profit and sales above Wall Street estimates.</a> Amazon shares rose by around 6% after the bell on Thursday.
