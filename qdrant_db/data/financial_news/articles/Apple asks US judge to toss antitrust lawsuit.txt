content: Apple <a href="https://www.reuters.com/markets/companies/AAPL.O" target="_blank">(AAPL.O)</a> on Thursday asked a U.S. judge to dismiss federal and state antitrust regulators' lawsuit accusing the iPhone maker of illegally monopolizing the smartphone market.
The Justice Department, 19 states and Washington, D.C., accuse Apple of an illegal monopoly on smartphones maintained by imposing contractual restrictions on, and withholding critical access from, developers.
